Stay Smart Online: Protect Yourself from Tricky Tricks!



What's Tricky Tricks Online?


Sometimes, people use tricky tricks to get others to share private stuff or do things they shouldn't.

We want to help you stay safe and smart online!


Tricks Examples:

Fishing for Secrets: Bad guys may try to trick you into sharing your secret codes or personal info.

Be careful!

Pretend Friends: Some people pretend to be your friend online to get you to trust them.

Always check if they're real!

Funny Games and Surveys: Be cautious with quizzes or games that ask for your secrets.

Real games won't ask for personal stuff.

Fake Contests: Watch out for contests that seem too good to be true.

They might just want your private details.



How to Stay Super Safe:

Ask Questions: If something seems weird or asks for secrets, ask a grown-up you trust if it's okay.

Double-Check Friends: Make sure the people you talk to online are really who they say they are.

Keep Secrets Safe: Your home, phone, and school info are like superhero secrets. Keep them safe from bad guys!

Strong and Secret Passwords: Create special passwords for your online stuff.
