Protect Yourself



Stranger Danger Online

Just like we don't talk to strangers on the street, we shouldn't talk to strangers online. Avoid sharing personal information like your name, address, school, or phone number.



Password Power!

Your password is like a secret code. Keep it strong and don't share it with anyone, not even friends. Use a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols.



Be a Detective!

Check if emails or messages are from real friends or family. Ask a grown-up if you're not sure. Be suspicious of messages asking for personal information or passwords.



Don't Click Everything!

Be careful with links and attachments. If you're unsure, check with a grown-up before clicking.



Privacy Settings Rock!

Use privacy settings on games and apps. Choose who can see your information and posts.



Be Kind Online

Treat others how you want to be treated. Cyberbullying is not cool. Tell a trusted adult if someone is being mean online.



Stay Updated!

Keep your devices and apps updated. This helps protect against bad stuff online.
